In this lesson, comic book cover artist Eric Gist (EMGIST) shares his journey in the industry. He discusses his favorite works, like covers for Fantastic Four and Hulk, and how he developed his style known for deep shadows and high contrast. Eric offers practical advice for aspiring artists on:

  • Studying both contemporary and historical artists to improve cover art.
  • Focusing on fundamentals like figure drawing, color theory, perspective, and composition.
  • Avoiding the trap of forcing a style and embracing your natural abilities.
  • Using limitations as a way to enhance creativity.
