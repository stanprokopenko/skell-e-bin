The Importance of Creativity

Drawing is a fundamental skill in nearly every creative field, but even more fundamental than drawing is creativity. The ability to think creatively and bring ideas from concept to reality is one of the most sought-after skills in a variety of fields.

What is Creativity?

A truly creative idea is both new and useful. To generate such ideas, you need to move beyond the ways you normally think and act. Creativity is not just a skill, it's a mindset, a way of looking at the world and a way of living life.

Developing a Creative Mindset

Embracing creativity involves finding connections between things and exploring meaning. By changing how you perceive the world, you can unlock new possibilities and ideas.

Generating Creative Ideas

Truly creative ideas are quite rare. The more ideas you generate, the more likely one of them will be creative. Learn how to generate numerous ideas and then evaluate them to figure out which ones you want to act on.

Turning Ideas into Projects

Discover a powerful process that teaches you how to turn any of your ideas into a project and how to complete that project. By systematically developing your ideas, you can bring them to fruition.

Overcoming Creative Blocks

Participate in guided journal projects where you write about your life and thoughts to get past your creative blocks and design your creative future. Reflecting on your experiences can help you find new inspiration.

No Experience Necessary

You don't need any art experience or special skills to become more creative. All you need is a desire to be more creative and a passion for a project you want to complete.

Applying Creativity to All Areas of Life

Whether you want to be more creative in your art, career, or personal life, learning essential creative skills will help you become more innovative and fulfilled.
