What's in Premium?

In this premium lesson, Eric Gist shares his portrait drawing techniques. You'll learn to start with the big shapes, using an envelope to establish accurate height-to-width ratios. Eric demonstrates how to separate the hair shape and face shape into general silhouettes, and how to find the center line to balance the features.

The lesson covers dividing the face into perfect thirds—brow line, nose line, and chin—and introduces rhythms inspired by Frank Riley to simplify facial structures. Eric guides you through the crucial two-value phase, simplifying the portrait into light and dark shapes to define your drawing effectively.

You'll explore how to refine your work by introducing additional values, building up to a five or six value drawing without overworking the piece. Eric also shares advice on focusing on fundamentals, developing a broad skill set, and understanding the sacrifices needed for artistic growth.

Finally, you'll learn how to add final touches, emphasizing the focal point like the eyes, refining details, and creating a balanced portrait that captures the essence of your subject.
