What's in Premium?

In this lesson, you'll learn two approaches to mastering form and proportions: the blob approach and the use of grids. The blob approach helps you capture forms quickly by focusing on line angles without worrying about proportions. Grids assist you in starting with accurate lines and comparing orthographic views (orthos) to foreshortened views, helping you guess proportions. You'll explore the benefits and challenges of using grids and learn from the techniques of artist Kim Jung Gi. The lesson includes a project where you'll redraw your form studies, practice foreshortening, and apply practical tips to improve your drawing skills.
