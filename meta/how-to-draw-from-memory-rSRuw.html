Visual Memory and Drawing from Imagination

Visual memory is challenging and exposes weaknesses in most people, including myself. Remembering major proportions, like height vs. width, is easier than smaller proportions. Constructing objects with correct proportions from imagination is difficult but essential for improving your drawing skills.

General Advice and Tips

Combining ellipses, boxes, cross contour lines, and construction techniques is essential. Freehanding lines improves the ability to draw straight lines at the right angle. Using a ruler can help learn perspective but may create dependency.
