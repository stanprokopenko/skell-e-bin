Engaging Head and Heart

Welcome to the program! We're embarking on a journey that engages both the head and the heart, blending craft and creativity, skill sets and mindset. This holistic approach will help you level up more quickly and less stressfully, guiding you towards finding your own unique creative voice.

Beyond Craft: The Essence of Art

While the laws of light, anatomy, and perspective are valuable tools, they are not art itself. Art is more than technical skills, it's about training our aesthetic and creative senses. By approaching these tools in a simpler, more childlike intuitive way, we can gain control more quickly, and feel less overwhelmed.

Art as a Connective Strategy

Our social systems often promote disconnection, leaving us feeling isolated. Art addresses this by dealing with heart-centered truths. It's a heart-centered, purpose-driven strategy that connects us to meaning and purpose.

Every craft choice is a connective strategy: connecting light to shadow, foreground to background, warm colors to cool. By thinking of art in this way, we simplify the process, making it easier, less stressful, and more effective.

The Healing Power of Art

Art and spiritual traditions are powerful wisdom traditions. They both address the trauma of disconnection and offer a path to healing. Through art, we get to spend our waking hours seeking beauty, which is a rare and special privilege.

Art has the power to:

  • Heal: Used in therapy for trauma and prison reform.
  • Connect: Helps us feel more connected and less isolated.
  • Inspire: Allows us to see the world in new and profound ways.

Craft and Creativity Together

We'll learn the skill of drawing the figure, but also dive into mindset and how to make overwhelming tasks manageable. By simplifying and chunking down complex processes, we gain more control and creative freedom.

Combining craft and creativity enables us to excel in our skills while expressing our own authentic truth. This approach helps us stand out and work from a place of genuine expression.
