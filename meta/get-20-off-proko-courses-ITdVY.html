The Unexpected Confrontation

Stan finds himself tied up in a mysterious room. A skeletal hand pulls a hood off his head, revealing Skelly standing before him.

"Skelly? What the hell!" exclaims Stan.

Skelly rotates ominously in a chair, his chin moving as he speaks. "I notice you've been cheaping out on animating me into videos lately," he accuses.

Stan responds, "Yeah, you're kind of expensive. What does this have to do with it?" He raises his tied hands.

Skelly's Heist Proposal

Skelly reveals his plan to fund his return by orchestrating a heist of the world's greatest art secrets. He slides a laptop toward Stan, displaying a wireframe of a building marked with red dots.

"Our goal is the server room," Skelly explains. "We take the 405 down to the main facility, enter through the back, and sneak past the guards posted here and here."

He continues detailing the plan, but his explanation becomes increasingly convoluted.

Stan's Realization

Underlit by the screen's glow, Stan listens, growing suspicious. He interrupts, "Wait. Is that my office? And that's our server. What the hell, man?"

A Trip Down Memory Lane

Skelly admits, "I just... I miss you guys. I want to be in the videos again. Like the good old days."

He reminisces about their past antics, recalling moments like Stan playfully spanking him. "And my favorite memory..." Skelly grins, recalling a time involving "Skelly boobs." He adds, "Sex appeal sells!"

Stan responds, "I have kids now."

Reaching an Agreement

"Can you untie me now?" Stan asks. "I promise you can be in a Drawing Basics episode. But no boobs!"

Skelly's mouth opens in a smile.
