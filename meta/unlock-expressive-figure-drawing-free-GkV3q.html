What's in Premium?

In this lesson, we'll focus on embracing gesture and rhythm in your figure drawings. We'll start by getting our hands limber to draw gestural limbs, building on the muscle memory you've developed. You'll learn how to simplify complex forms using mnemonics like thinking of the rib cage as a tube with an egg around it or the pelvis as a Liberty Bell.

We'll emphasize capturing the flow and gesture over mechanical structure, keeping your lines long, light, and loose. Understanding where the body pinches and stretches in different poses adds life to your drawings. You'll practice using the underhand grip to create expressive marks and achieve fluidity in your lines.

The lesson also explores incorporating story and emotion into your figures, making your drawings more engaging. We'll reflect on lessons from art history, drawing inspiration from great artists. Above all, we'll focus on simplicity, removing unnecessary details to convey the essence of the figure. Through experimentation and practice, you'll enjoy the journey of improving your figure drawing skills.
