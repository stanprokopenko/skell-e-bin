What's in Premium?

In this premium lesson, we focus on Visual Memory Games Level Two: Mannequinized Figure Drawing. You'll learn three exercises designed to improve your visual memory and ability to draw figures from imagination:

  1. Drawing from Memory: Observe a pose, put the reference away, and draw it from memory. You'll learn how to analyze gestures, rhythms, proportions, and alignments to capture the figure accurately.

  2. Drawing from a Different Angle: Draw the pose from the same angle, then, without the reference, draw it again from a different viewpoint. This exercise enhances your understanding of three-dimensional forms and spatial relationships.

  3. Changing Angle and Proportions: Study a pose and draw it from a new angle with altered proportions. This challenges you to adapt and design figures while maintaining coherence.

By practicing these exercises, you'll enhance your ability to visualize and construct figures, improve your understanding of forms, and build confidence in drawing from imagination.
