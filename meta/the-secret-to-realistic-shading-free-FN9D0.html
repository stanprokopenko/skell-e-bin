Learn how to shade your drawings to create realistic depth. This lesson explains how light interacts with forms and how understanding planes—the surfaces that make up an object—helps in shading.

  • Form shadow

The lesson guides you on establishing a value structure by grouping values into the light family and shadow family. You'll learn to use limited value groups to simplify shading and convey three-dimensional form effectively.
