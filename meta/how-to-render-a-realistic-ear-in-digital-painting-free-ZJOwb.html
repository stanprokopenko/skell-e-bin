What's in Premium?

In this premium lesson, you'll learn to paint a full color rendering of an ear, step by step. We start with a color wash using cadmium red-orange to set a warm base. You'll create a rough gesture drawing, using specific shapes like the hook shape for the helix and antihelix, and organize your work with layers.

You'll learn how to block in flat local colors for the skin, hair, and earrings, and how to separate lights and darks to define shadow shapes. The lesson covers adding midtones and highlights, refining edges with blending techniques, and incorporating subsurface scattering to mimic light under the skin. We'll guide you through adding dark accents and highlights to enhance depth, and applying final touches to bring your painting together. Additionally, we'll revisit ear anatomy to reinforce your understanding of each part you're rendering.
