Get into the Routine of Drawing Daily

Challenge yourself to draw every day. See if you can get into the routine of drawing daily. Set aside a time. Put it in your calendar.

Increase Your Challenge

If you're already drawing every day, then do something to challenge yourself a little more. Draw a bit longer. But don't go too crazy. Set a manageable goal because this is in addition to the other project I'll be giving you later.

Daily Assignment: Fill Up One Page with Linear Drawings

Every day, fill up one page with linear drawings from life. You can either go outside and draw something when inspiration hits or find something at home and draw it. Take a photo of your objects.

Drawing Tips

If you can, start by just looking at your subject for one minute. Imagine it as a drawing. Draw a few lines. Then look. Think and execute. Repeat until you're done.

Each drawing can take 5 to 30 minutes, but don't time them.

I'll Be Drawing Along with You

I'll be drawing along with you guys and posting in the discussions below.
