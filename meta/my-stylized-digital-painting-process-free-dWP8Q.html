What's in Premium?

Learn how to take an illustration from a sketch to a finished rendering in digital painting. I share my process:

  • Start with a Sketch: Focus on the largest shapes, keeping it loose and stylized.
  • Add Values: Build up shadows to define the forms.
  • Add Color Using Layer Modes: Apply color over the values.
  • Merge Layers and Paint on Top: Refine the painting by merging layers and adding details.

Understand how to think in both 2D shapes and 3D forms, and how to iterate and refine your sketch to build a solid foundation.
