In this lesson, I share my journey in the comics industry, starting with my work on Alpha Flight, Longshot, and Punisher, leading up to the X-Books and co-founding Image Comics. I discuss how mentorship from Karl Potts at Marvel taught me the importance of storytelling and focus in art.

You'll learn about transitioning roles—from inking to penciling and writing—and how embracing both digital and traditional techniques can enhance your creativity. I explain how to direct the reader's eye by choosing a focal point in your scenes, and how combining digital layouts with traditional inking provides freedom in the creative process.

I also dive into creating meaningful art that resonates with people, sharing insights from covers like Wolverine and a personal favorite featuring Storm. This lesson emphasizes the power of storytelling, even in a single image, and how infusing emotion and backstory can make your artwork stand out.
