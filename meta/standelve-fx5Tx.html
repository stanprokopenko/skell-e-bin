Verb Forms and Usage

Dive is a verb with forms dives, dove, and diving.

  • She dove into the matter.
  • He dives into the code.
  • Diving deeper, she found more.
  • Diving is fun!

Capitalization Rules

At the beginning of a sentence, Diving is capitalized. In the middle of a sentence, diving is lowercase.
