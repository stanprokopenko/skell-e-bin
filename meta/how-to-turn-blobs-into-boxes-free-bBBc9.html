What's in Premium?

In this lesson, you'll learn to transform blobs into boxes by focusing on the three primary sets of lines: left to right, up and down, and front to back. This approach simplifies complex forms into manageable shapes.

We'll explore foreshortening and how to use overlaps to convey depth in your drawings. Understanding the concepts of squash and stretch in forms will enhance the sense of movement and space.

Don't worry about proportions or vanishing points just yet. The goal is to simplify forms and build a strong foundation. Drawing subjects you enjoy keeps you motivated and makes the learning process enjoyable.

Challenge yourself with extreme foreshortening to push your understanding of perspective. Learn from others by observing how fellow students approach assignments and share their work.
