What's in Premium?

In this lesson, we focus on the lightness of touch in drawing. You'll learn how reducing the pressure on your pencil enhances your lines, making them more delicate and expressive.

We dive into how light affects drawing, influencing mood and depth through lines and tones. You'll understand how to break heavy lines into thinner ones to tell a different story.

Maintaining the flow of the figure is crucial. You'll explore techniques to keep your drawings lively and dynamic, regardless of the subject.

We introduce simple shapes and mnemonics to simplify complex forms:

  • Liberty Bell for the pelvis
  • Egg for the ribcage
  • Tube for limbs
  • Mr. T and Mrs. V for facial features

You'll learn to use a light touch when adding tone, enhancing your work without overwhelming it. We also discuss drawing faces and expressions, adding rhythm through line work and minimal tone.

The lesson highlights the value of life drawing classes, helping you see reality more clearly and improve your skills. You'll discover the importance of continuous practice and connecting with fellow artists.
