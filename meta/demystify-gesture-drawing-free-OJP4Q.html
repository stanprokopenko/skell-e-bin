What's in Premium?

In the "Understanding Gesture Drawing" course, Stephen Michael Hampton focuses on making gesture drawing accessible by organizing your thoughts and demystifying the process. You'll learn:

  • How to pose your figures effectively
  • Thinking about the internal dynamics of your figures
  • Understanding proportion in your drawings
  • Key concepts of design in figure drawing
  • Adjusting your figures based on your priorities for the final result

The course provides structure and steps while allowing for different styles, helping you understand why you're doing gesture drawing and how it fits into your artistic process. By the end, you'll have new approaches to gesture and a broad understanding to innovate in your own work.
