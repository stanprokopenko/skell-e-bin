Learn an observational approach to drawing the figure by designing each part as a simple shape. In this lesson, we'll break down the figure into basic parts—head, neck, torso, arms, and legs—and explore the gesture of each. You'll learn how to apply simple perspective using wrapping lines and add light and shadow to bring out the form. We'll dive into techniques for designing the head and neck, modifying shapes to convey different characters and expressions. By connecting the parts and employing light and shadow, you'll create figures that are structurally sound and have a strong sense of character. This method is particularly beneficial if you have some anatomy knowledge and want to get to light and shadow more directly in your figure drawings.
