What's in Premium?

In the premium lesson "My Sketchbook Practice: Building a Visual Library", I share how I use my sketchbook as a practice zone to study from reference and memorize material. You'll learn how to fill your sketchbook with sketches of plants, everyday objects, and more, drawing from life and resources like Pinterest.

I explain how to build a visual library by drawing many versions of the same thing, which helps you avoid generic drawings. We discuss how to tell stories through objects, enhancing your art by adding personality and history to items like chairs and lamps.

You'll see how I learn from other artists by studying their work, and how working in pen helps me embrace mistakes and improve. I cover practicing compositions, arranging objects with attention to shape and balance. Finally, I show how these studies allow me to draw from imagination, storing knowledge internally to bring more depth to my art.
