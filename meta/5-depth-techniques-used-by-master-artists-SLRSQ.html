Overlap

Atmospheric Perspective Explored

To elaborate on atmospheric perspective, remember the three Cs:

  • Contrast: Distant objects have less contrast compared to foreground elements.
  • Color Changes: Colors shift as objects recede, such as green mountains appearing more lavender in the distance.
  • Clarity of Detail: Details become less clear with distance, nearby objects show more texture and detail.

Understanding these components allows you to effectively apply atmospheric perspective to create depth in your work.
