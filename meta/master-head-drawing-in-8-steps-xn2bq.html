Master Head Drawing in 8 Steps

Copy Code

For homework, aim to complete all eight steps.

Step 1: Gesture - Focus on the shape of the skull.

Step 2: Determine the Tilt - Identify the two-dimensional axes.

Step 3: Draw the Jaw and Cross - Combine the center line and brow.

Step 4: Establish Perspective - Examine the brow's relationship to the ear.

Step 5: Discuss Proportions - Mark halves and thirds between the brow and chin.

Step 6: Develop the Side Plane - Use four points, including the ear, jaw, and cheek.

Step 7: Introduce the Eye Sockets - Start with a planar visor. - Move to a more complex three-tiered plane.

Step 8: Develop the Nose - From the keystone to the tip, and then to the base. - Consider the large area of the denture sphere and the chin.

Homework Assignment

Drawing Practice - Draw 20 heads. - Half should build on prior assignments, focusing on steps up to five or six. - The other half should start new, working through all steps.

Time Management - Aim for each drawing to be a 10-minute exercise. - Extending to 15 minutes is acceptable.

Goals - Encourage repetition to ingrain the process into thinking and muscle memory. - Avoid over-focusing on details to create routine and habit through practice.

Feedback - Your work will be critiqued.
