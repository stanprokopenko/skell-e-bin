Getting Started

For this lesson's assignment, you will be given some figure references. You're now exploring the part-to-part approach, incorporating gesture, perspective, and working from head to foot.

Embracing the Part-to-Part Approach

When working on these drawings, make sure to have patience and grace with yourself. Don't be too hard on yourself, it's going to be uncomfortable at first, especially if you've been using the other approach. This is your chance to reorganize your thoughts and redevelop a new way of representing and organizing the same information you've been looking at.

Tips for Success

  • Treat it with levity and have fun.
  • If you're finding it difficult, try playing around with the exaggeration of the parts and modifying some forms. Experimenting with a little exaggeration might help you connect with the approach a bit more.
