Testing OpenAI's Deep Research

OpenAI has been active lately, so I tested their latest offering: Deep Research. Similar to Google's Gemini but powered by the 03 model, it's designed for in-depth analysis. I wanted to see its creative potential.

Despite its $200/month price tag, I tried it out and packaged the reports for you to explore.

Comparing AI Video Generators

I asked Deep Research: What's the best AI video generator for quality and cost? It generated a detailed report after clarifying questions.

Findings

  • Minimax: Produces 720p videos with vibrant colors but limited control.
  • CLLING: Excellent prompt adherence, occasional glitches, slightly less polished than Runway.
  • Runway: Fast generation, robust editing tools, but inconsistent prompt control.
  • Sora: Promising but struggles with realism and complex actions, not ideal for budgets.

Conclusion

Deep Research found CLLING offers the best quality-to-price ratio, with Runway close behind. Kudos to OpenAI for unbiased results.

Subscriptions vs. Local Setup

I also analyzed whether subscriptions or building a local machine is more cost-effective.

Costs

  • Subscriptions: Around $3,100/year
  • Local Machine:
    • Hardware: $3,300
    • Training: $2,000
    • Electricity: $350/year

Conclusion

Initially, a local machine costs more but becomes cost-effective over time. Most of us use a hybrid approach.
