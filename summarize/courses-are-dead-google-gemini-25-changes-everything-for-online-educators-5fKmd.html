Courses Are Dead? Google Gemini 2.5 Changes Everything for Online Educators

Copy Code

Google did it again. With the release of Google Gemini 2.5 Pro Experimental on May 6th, they're making waves in the world of AI and education. This new update allows anyone to build rich interactive web apps using just a couple of sentences or prompts. As someone deeply involved in creating educational content, I can't help but see how this might revolutionize the way we approach course creation.

Building Interactive Web Apps with Gemini 2.5 Pro

The standout feature of Gemini 2.5 Pro is its ability to create standalone web apps, which are deployable in an HTML page. These aren't just static pages, they're interactive and can perform a variety of tasks. For example, I asked Gemini to create a web app that teaches how to make coffee. With a simple prompt, it generated an interactive tutorial, complete with steps and visuals.

Coffee Making Tutorial Example

By prompting Gemini with "Create a web app that teaches me how to make coffee," it produced an interactive guide. Users can click through each step, from grinding the beans to enjoying the final brew. This hands-on approach engages learners more effectively than traditional text or video content.

How Web Apps Can Replace Traditional Courses

Traditional courses often consist of videos, text lessons, quizzes, and perhaps some interactive elements. But with these AI-generated web apps, we can create learning experiences that are far more engaging. They require user interaction, ensuring that learners are actively participating rather than passively consuming information.

Sequencing Applications

I also experimented with creating a sequencing app. By providing the steps of a process, Gemini generated an application where learners can arrange the steps in the correct order. When they check their work, the app provides feedback on their accuracy. This kind of interactivity reinforces learning in a way static content can't.

Applications for Personal Skills and Small Businesses

Personal Skills

Think about skills like photo editing for social media or cooking the perfect scrambled eggs. Instead of a series of lessons or a recipe, we can provide an interactive experience. For cooking scrambled eggs, I had Gemini create an interactive tutorial that guides the user through each step, ensuring they understand and complete each part of the process.

Small Business Processes

For small businesses, these web apps can serve as standard operating procedures. Imagine onboarding new employees with interactive guides on handling customer inquiries or creating social media posts. These apps ensure consistency and engagement, making training more effective.

The Future of Multimodal AI

Gemini 2.5 Pro isn't just about text-based prompts. With the integration of multimodal AI, we can interact using text, voice, and visuals. For instance, you could use your phone to show Gemini the contents of your fridge, and it could suggest recipes and provide interactive cooking guides on the spot.

Implications for Course Creators

As someone who has created countless courses, this technology is both exciting and challenging. It prompts us to ask: Do we need to create traditional courses anymore? With the ability to generate interactive, engaging content quickly, perhaps it's time to rethink our approach.

These web apps are:

  • Easy to create: Simple prompts can generate complex applications.
  • Interactive: They require user engagement, enhancing learning.
  • Versatile: Applicable for various subjects and skills.

* * *

The advancements in AI with tools like Google Gemini 2.5 Pro are reshaping the educational landscape. As educators and content creators, we need to embrace these technologies to provide the best learning experiences possible. It's an exciting time, and I can't wait to see how we can leverage these tools to better teach and engage our audiences.
