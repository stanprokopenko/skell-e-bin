Remarkable Growth with Aggressive Marketing

Hims and Hers is experiencing rapid, profitable growth. In their latest quarter, they earned $400 million in revenue with 77% year-on-year growth, making $76 million in profit.

I've noticed they invest almost half their revenue in marketing, about $182 million per quarter. By investing in profitable marketing activities, they're able to scale aggressively. They know they'll get more out than they put in.

Simple, Mobile-First Website

Their website is simple and mobile-first, tailored for visitors already familiar with Hims. It swiftly guides users into a consultative process, increasing engagement by delaying lead capture until the end.

Their Good Health blog drives significant organic traffic. By covering health topics related to their treatments and optimizing content, they effectively funnel readers to their products.

Strong Search Presence

Through heavy investment in content marketing and SEO, they drive around 1 million organic visitors per month. Their topic cluster approach and strong backlink profile boost their search rankings.

In paid search, they spend about $500,000 per month on desktop ads, focusing on brand terms with benefits-focused messaging.

Effective Social and Video Advertising

They allocate significant budget to social and video ads, favoring high-quality video content over solely UGC-style ads. Their top-performing ads are consistent in branding and messaging, emphasizing benefits to build familiarity.

Despite this, I've noticed their organic video content on platforms like YouTube and TikTok underperforms, revealing an area for improvement.

Lessons Learned

  • Strong Core Offering: Simple, competitively priced solutions addressing specific needs.
  • User-Friendly Website: Mobile-first design with a consultative approach that smoothly guides users.
  • Content and SEO Investment: High-quality, optimized content driving significant organic traffic.
  • Aggressive Ad Strategy: Large ad spend with extensive testing and focus on high-performing video ads.
  • Scaling Profitable Models: Rapid scaling once a profitable marketing strategy is found.

Despite their massive success, they still have opportunities to improve, especially in their organic social video content.
