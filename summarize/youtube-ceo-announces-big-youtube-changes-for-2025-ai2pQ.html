More Ways to Earn on YouTube

Making a steady income as a YouTuber can be tough, especially if ad revenue is your only source. Good news: YouTube is introducing new ways for you to earn money beyond ads. Last year, over 50% of channels earning five figures or more made money from sources other than ads and YouTube Premium.

New Opportunities

  • Improved Channel Memberships: Engage your community with exclusive perks.
  • Enhanced Shopping Features: Sell products directly through your channel.
  • Brand Partnerships: Explore new ways to collaborate with brands.
  • Launching Courses: Sell lessons directly on YouTube, similar to Skillshare, and keep most of the revenue.

Embracing Podcasts

YouTube is now the most frequently used service for listening to podcasts in the US. To support this trend, YouTube is introducing:

  • New Tools for Podcasters: Enhance your podcast production and reach.
  • Improved Monetization: Earn more from your podcast content.
  • Easier Discovery: Help listeners find your podcasts more easily.

The Shift to TV Viewing

For the first time, YouTube TV has surpassed mobile as the primary device for viewing in the US. This shift means more watch time and opportunities for you.

Creating for TV Audiences

To optimize your content for TV viewers, consider these strategies:

  1. Be Sharp: Use 4K video and high-resolution thumbnails for a crisp look on big screens.
  2. Be Bingeable: Create longer, compelling stories and series that keep viewers watching.
  3. Be Browsable: Organize your channel page to feature your freshest and most popular content at the top.

Leveraging AI Tools

AI is transforming how you create content by enhancing your workflow.

AI Empowerment

  • Auto-Dubbing: Translate your videos into multiple languages with a touch of a button. For videos with dubbed audio, over 40% of watch time comes from viewers choosing a dubbed language.
  • Dream Track: Generate unique soundtracks for your Shorts using text prompts.
  • Dream Screen: Create AI-generated image and video backgrounds.
  • AI Brainstorming Tools: Use the Inspiration tab in YouTube Studio for video outlines, title ideas, and thumbnail concepts.

Interactive TV Features

YouTube is testing new features to enhance the TV viewing experience:

  • Second Screen Experience: Allow users to interact with videos via their phones.
  • Watch With: Provide live commentary and real-time reactions to games and events.

Stay tuned for these exciting updates in 2025. Consider how you can adapt your content strategy to make the most of these new opportunities.
