Overview

Creating a newsletter can be time-consuming. By automating the process using AI tools, you can save hours each week. This guide shows you how to use Perplexity AI, Claude, and Make.com to streamline your newsletter creation.

Tools You'll Need

  • Perplexity AI: For up-to-date desk research with internet access.
  • Claude: An AI assistant for content generation.
  • Make.com: An automation platform to connect everything.
  • Google Sheets: To organize your ideas and outputs.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Prepare Your Google Sheet

  • Create a Sheet: List your newsletter topics, outlines, and target audience.
  • Add Details: Include any specific instructions or maximum word counts.

2. Use Perplexity AI for Research

  • Internet Access: Perplexity AI fetches current information with citations.
  • Generate Summaries: Ask it to summarize the latest on your chosen topic.
  • Collect Sources: Save any useful links or references provided.

3. Generate Content with Claude

  • Create a Project: In Claude, start a new project to add custom instructions.
  • Set System Prompts: Define your newsletter style, tone, and audience.
  • Feed Context: Include the research from Perplexity AI in your prompts.
  • Generate Drafts: Ask Claude to write your newsletter content.

4. Automate Workflow with Make.com

  • Set Up a Scenario: In Make.com, create an automation pipeline.
  • Connect Google Sheets: Watch for new rows (topics) added to your sheet.
  • Integrate Perplexity AI: Automate desk research for each topic.
  • Connect Claude: Automate content generation with your custom prompts.
  • Add Other Steps:
    • Headline Writer: Generate multiple headlines to choose from.
    • Image Search: Use Unsplash API to find relevant images.
    • News Bulletin: Include additional AI-generated news snippets.

5. Execute and Review

  • Run the Automation: Execute your Make.com scenario.
  • Check Outputs: Review the generated content in your Google Sheet.
  • Edit as Needed: Make any necessary tweaks to ensure quality and accuracy.
  • Finalize: Compile the content, headlines, and images into your newsletter.

Tips for Success

  • Customize Prompts: Tailor AI prompts to match your brand voice.
  • Monitor AI Limitations - Remember that AI may lack the latest data, always fact-check.
  • Leverage Projects in Claude: Use projects to maintain context across sessions.
  • Save Time: By automating repetitive tasks, focus on adding personal touches.

* * *

By integrating Perplexity AI, Claude, and Make.com, you can automate up to 90% of your newsletter creation process. This approach leverages AI for research and writing while allowing you to maintain control over the final content. Start saving time and enhancing your newsletters today!
