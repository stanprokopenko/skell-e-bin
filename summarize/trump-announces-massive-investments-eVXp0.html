Major Investment in Arizona

Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) announced an investment of $165 billion to build five cutting-edge semiconductor fabrication facilities in Arizona. Mr. CC Wei, TSMC's CEO, stated that they will produce the most advanced chips in the world, including AI chips and components for smartphones.

Creating Thousands of High-Paying Jobs

This investment will create thousands of high-paying jobs in Arizona. With 3,000 employees already working, TSMC plans to expand further, contributing to significant economic activity and boosting the local economy.

Boosting America's Technological Dominance

Semiconductors are the backbone of the 21st-century economy, powering everything from AI to automobiles. We believe that producing these essential components domestically will boost America's dominance in artificial intelligence and beyond.

Enhancing Economic and National Security

Building chips in American factories with American skill and labor is vital for our economic and national security. President Trump emphasized, "Without the semiconductors, there is no economy," highlighting the importance of reducing reliance on foreign production.

Collaboration with Major Tech Companies

TSMC's investment is supported by major U.S. tech companies such as Apple, NVIDIA, AMD, Qualcomm, and Broadcom. Their support ensures that the most advanced chips will be produced in the U.S., strengthening our technological infrastructure and supply chain.
