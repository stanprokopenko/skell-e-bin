Vice President Harris Advocates Eliminating the Filibuster

Vice President Kamala Harris has taken a strong stance on reproductive rights. She expressed willingness to eliminate the filibuster to protect these rights, stating, "I think we should eliminate the filibuster and get us to the point where 51 votes would be what we need to actually put back in law the protections for reproductive freedom." Harris emphasized the importance of allowing every woman to make decisions about her own body without government interference.
