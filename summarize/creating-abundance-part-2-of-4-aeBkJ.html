Understanding the Abundance Mindset

Reality responds to your vibration, the energetic signature you're putting out into the world. When you emanate an abundance vibe, your reality brings you experiences that match that state. You attract -

  • Massive opportunities in business.
  • Plentiful friendships and loving relationships.
  • Joyful and growth-filled experiences.
  • Plenty of money flowing through your life.
  • The ability to always afford what you want without ever having to say, "I can't afford it."

Conversely, when you put out a scarcity vibe, you attract:

  • Bills bigger than your paycheck.
  • Overdue payments.
  • Massive debt that feels disempowering.

By giving your power away to circumstances beyond your control, you're telling reality, "I am powerless." And reality responds accordingly.

Shifting Your Vibration to Abundance

Focus on Gratitude and Desires

Start by focusing on the things you're grateful for in life. Redirect your attention to the experiences you want. Withdraw power from negativity and put it back out in a positive form.

A Practical Process to Create an Abundance Vibe

Here's a formal process you can use to shift your vibration. While simple to do, it might be challenging to accept that reality works this way. But remember, you're in a matrix of sorts that responds to your vibration.

Step 1: Set Up Your Visualization

Sit or lie down comfortably, on your couch or bed. Close your eyes.

Step 2: Visualize Through Your Own Eyes

Imagine the new reality you want to experience. See it as real and feeling good, as if you're already there. Visualize fully associated, seeing everything through your own eyes, not as an outsider looking in.

The visualization is a tool to create the vibration, the feeling.

Step 3: Feel the Emotions of Abundance

Ask yourself: If I was already there, how would I really feel about reality?

Imagine different scenarios:

  • Money flowing through your life.
  • Going shopping with massive amounts of cash, buying whatever you want.
  • Your bank account always refilling, even after spending.
  • Living in your dream home.
  • Traveling wherever you want, on a moment's notice.
  • Having loving relationships and close friendships.
  • Experiencing intimacy and connectedness with people you care about.

Focus on the feelings:

  • Feeling so lucky and fortunate.
  • Waking up and going to bed feeling grateful.
  • Experiencing such an amazing life that you might even feel moved to tears.

Step 4: Practice Regularly

The first time you do this, you might only lock into that emotional signature for 30 seconds or a minute. It might take time to visualize in a way that elicits those feelings.

But keep at it. Do it for 20 minutes every day.

The goal is to get good at locking onto that emotional feeling. Eventually, you'll figure out the right visualization that makes you feel truly abundant.

Step 5: Extend the Feeling Beyond Visualization

As you practice, you'll start to feel that vibration elsewhere in your life. Even when you're not doing these visualization exercises, that abundance state will get triggered.

Those 20 minutes are a practice session, a conditioning session.

Step 6: Incorporate It Into Your Daily Routine

You can do this anytime:

  • While driving - think about the feelings and situations you want to experience.
  • Standing in line - allow yourself to feel abundance.
  • Waking up in the morning - create that feeling right as you get up.
  • Going to bed - make it a priority to feel abundance as you're drifting off to sleep.

The more often you do this, the better. Sometimes, you might set aside an hour or more to deeply engage in this practice.

Shifting Your Dominant Vibration

After several weeks or a few months, depending on how you practice and any opposing influences, you'll start to feel these feelings more frequently. Eventually, you'll reach a point where, more than half the time, you're feeling these abundance emotions.

The goal is to shift your dominant vibration, so you're going through most of the day feeling abundant. This becomes your default state of being.

You'll wake up feeling happy, excited about life, and passionate. You'll interact with friends feeling joyful. You won't feel frustrated by unmet desires because you'll know you can create the life you want.

* * *

By consistently practicing this process, you're conditioning yourself to feel abundant regularly. Reality responds by aligning opportunities and experiences that match your new vibration. You'll find yourself living a life where abundance is the norm, and creating the experiences you desire becomes a natural part of who you are.
